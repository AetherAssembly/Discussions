## 📋 Code of Conduct

### Our Commitment

AetherAssembly is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community for all members. We value diverse perspectives, constructive dialogue, and collaborative problem-solving.

### Expected Behavior

✅ **Do:**
- Be respectful and inclusive of all members
- Use clear, constructive language
- Assume good intent in others' messages
- Listen actively and consider different viewpoints
- Give credit where due
- Help others and share knowledge generously
- Stay on-topic within discussion categories
- Report concerns privately if needed

❌ **Don't:**
- Use offensive, discriminatory, or harassing language
- Post spam, promotional content, or off-topic advertisements
- Engage in personal attacks or ad hominem arguments
- Share private information without consent
- Derail discussions or be intentionally disruptive
- Plagiarize or claim credit for others' work
- Cross-post the same message repeatedly

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:
- Hate speech, discrimination, or harassment based on identity
- Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexual advances
- Threats, doxxing, or encouraging harm
- Sharing private information ("doxing")
- Persistent off-topic behavior after being asked to refocus

### Enforcement

- **First Violation:** Warning and request to modify behavior
- **Second Violation:** Temporary mute or suspension
- **Severe or Repeated Violations:** Permanent removal from the community

Maintainers reserve the right to remove discussions, comments, or members at their discretion to maintain a safe and respectful space.

### Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:
- Use GitHub's built-in report feature on the comment/discussion
- Email [support@aetherassembly.org](mailto:support@aetherassembly.org) with details
- Contact a maintainer directly on GitHub
